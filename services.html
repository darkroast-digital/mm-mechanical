Ongoing Maintenance Prevents Emergencies

In addition to emergency repairs, our Maintenance Program offers services to maintan the peak performance of your system. It's easy, it's convinient and it ensures the high efficiency of your system for years! It's recommended that you service your heating and cooling system twice a year, once for heating and once for cooling, in order to maintain proper efficiency and peak performance. Efficiency and peak performance keeps your energy down and money in your pocket.

  • Condenser coils will be inspected for heat transfer loss.
  • Fans will be inspected and cleaned to assure proper air delivery.
  • Refrigerant will be checked for proper charge and to assure there are no leaks.
  • Exposed duct work will be inspected for leaks.
  • Thermostats will be checked and calibrated as necessary
  • Motors and bearings will be lubricated as needed.
  • the condensate drain will be checked.
  • The crankcase heater will be checked for proper operation.
  • Relays and contacts will be inspected.
  • Humidifiers will be checked to ensure maximum operating efficiency.
  • Temperatures and pressures will be checked and recorded.
  • The evaporator coil will be inspected.
  • Aire filters will be regularly replaced.
